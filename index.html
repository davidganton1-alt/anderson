About the Firm

Resolving Complex International Claims

Anderson International is a specialist legal practice focused exclusively on cross-border insolvency and international financial claims recovery. We operate from Chancery House on Chancery Lane, London, the historic centre of the English legal profession.

Our work centres on cases where assets are trapped in foreign insolvency proceedings, where creditors have been unable to navigate complex jurisdictional requirements, or where the passage of time has created procedural barriers that require expert legal intervention.

We combine deep expertise in English and international insolvency law with established local counsel networks to deliver results in proceedings spanning multiple jurisdictions. Every case is handled with the rigour, discretion, and attention to detail that complex international disputes demand.

Our practice also encompasses cross-border probate and estate administration, including matters where beneficiaries reside outside the jurisdiction of the probate court. We have handled complex international succession cases involving UK, EU, and Commonwealth estate assets.

Practice Areas

Specialist Expertise

Cross-Border Insolvency

Navigating complex insolvency proceedings across multiple jurisdictions, including civil rehabilitation, Chapter 15, and UNCITRAL Model Law cases. We coordinate with local counsel to protect creditor interests wherever proceedings arise.

Financial Claims Recovery

Recovering assets from failed financial institutions, exchanges, and investment platforms. We specialize in claims requiring blockchain forensic evidence, ledger discrepancy analysis, and deposit tracing across custodial systems.

Belated Creditor Claims

Pursuing time-barred or belated claims where creditors were unable to participate in proceedings due to medical incapacity, lack of notice, or circumstances beyond their control. Expert in petition drafting and court applications for late admission.

Digital Asset Disputes

Specialist experience in cryptocurrency exchange insolvency proceedings, including Mt. Gox and successor cases. On-chain forensic analysis, deposit verification, and trustee engagement strategies.

Japanese Proceedings

Established co-counsel relationships with licensed bengoshi in Tokyo. Direct experience with the Civil Rehabilitation Act, Tokyo District Court procedures, and creditor petition processes under Japanese law.

Forensic Evidence & Analysis

Commissioning and coordinating certified forensic blockchain affidavits, medical expert reports, and technical evidence packages. Building court-ready cases with independently verifiable evidence.

Probate & Estate Administration

Assisting executors, administrators, and beneficiaries with domestic and cross-border estate administration. We handle asset collection and appraisal, preparation of court filings, distribution of estate property, and the location and notification of beneficiaries across jurisdictions including UK, EU, and Commonwealth estates.

Our Approach

How We Work

Every engagement follows a structured, transparent process designed to give clients clarity from the outset.

01

Preliminary Assessment

We review the facts, analyse the legal framework, assess viability, and provide a detailed written assessment before any commitment is made. No obligation, no pressure.

02

Strategic Engagement

Clear engagement terms with defined scope, transparent fee structures, and no hidden costs. We coordinate all local counsel, translations, court filings, and procedural steps.

03

Execution & Recovery

We prosecute the claim to resolution, keeping you informed at every stage. Our contingency-based model means our interests are fully aligned with yours throughout the proceedings.
